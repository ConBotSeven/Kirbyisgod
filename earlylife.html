Where did Kirby come from?

Kirby's true origins are unknown, but it sometimes is stated that Void, who is the God of the Kirby universe, created him after coming across a lot of positive emotions. He just showed up on Popstar one day and he's lived there ever since.

Kirby's Friends

Kirby doesn't have any known family members, but he has a few friends such as Bandana Waddle Dee, King Dedede, and Meta Knight, who typically only challenge Kirby when they're under the influence of evil or just for a friendly battle. They have all been with him since the beginning. There are more friends of his, such as Rick, Kine, Coo, and Gooey but it would take a while to list them all.

Kirby's Early Influences

Kirby didn't really have any early influences, he is just the embodiment of good and goes to fight evil in the first game.
